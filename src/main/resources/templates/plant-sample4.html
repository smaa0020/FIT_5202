    Plant name: Boston Fern

     

    Introduction:

    Ferns are a popular indoor plant. Boston ferns need a cool place with high humidity and indirect light. Might need additional humidity in the winters. Leaves will turn yellow if the humidity is not high enough. Ideally indoor ferns will need some humidity and some indirect light. Suitable temperatures are 15-21 degrees Celsius. They do not need too much fertiliser.

     

    Plant name: Lipstick plant

     

    Introduction:

    Provided they get enough warmth, humidity, and filtered sunlight, you'll enjoy a prolific display of flowering through much of the year. Generally, though, lipstick plants flower most abundantly in the summer and fall.

     

    Plant name: Parsley

     

    Introduction:

    This is a very common herb, which can also be used for interior decoration. It not only makes people feel refreshed, but the fresh fragrance of vegetation can also relieve people’s fatigue.

     

    Plant name: Rosemary

     

    Introduction:

    The fragrant plant is a delightful scent and is sometimes used in floral arrangements. Rosemary likes its soil a bit on the dry side, so be careful not to overwater. Allowed to flourish, a rosemary plant will grow into a full-sized bush.

     

    Plant name: Bird Nest Fern

     

    Introduction:

    The Bird’s Nest Fern has large simple fronds that are tropical-like in that they resemble banana leaves. Their fronds are light green, often crinkled or wavy, and rise from a central rosette. The rosette is fuzzy where the emerging fronds resemble bird’s eggs - hence the common name.
