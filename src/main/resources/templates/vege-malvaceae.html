    Tips to protect


    1. Loosening the soil and weeding can maintain moisture content and increase the ground temperature to promote rooting and survival, but it is necessary to prevent root damage and shoot damage.

    2. The elm tree is most afraid of the soil being too wet. Make sure that the soil is slightly dry and don’t accumulate water.
