    Recommended Activities


    There are some popular public welfare activities about protecting urban nature area.


    Nurtured by Nature Access Program

    Nurtured by Nature Access Program which aims to improve access to our gardens for Victorian students through one-off subsidised excursions, digital garden experiences and longer-term partnerships.

    Official Website
    Gardens for Wildlife

    Gardens for Wildlife Melbourne aims to inspire Melburnians to create space for nature in the city.

    Official Website
    Volunteer with eco centre

    An empowered community, actively cultivating long-term environmental well-being​. They have different volunteering programs-Compost Turners, Garden Waters, NatureSpot, Mollusc Monitoring.

    Official Website
    Wildlife gardening

    Garden in a way that supports the unique and special plants and animals native to our area – what we call wildlife gardening.

    Official Website
    Nature photo contest

    Helping make our world greener and more sustainable.

    Official Website
    Melbourne Zoo Ticket

    Located in the heart of the city, you can discover penguins, seals and other inhabitants of Victoria's coast and ocean at the Wild Sea exhibition

    Official Website
    Penguins in St Kilda

    Home to 1,400 penguins, St Kilda’s sandy beaches are the perfect spot to see some wildlife without having to travel too far from Melbourne’s CBD. But be sure not to touch them, feed them, or shine any lights in their eyes! We want to protect our little tuxedoed friends.

    Official Website
