    Plant name: Fern

     

    Introduction:

    Ferns are a popular indoor plant. Ferns in hanging pots/tubs are a very attractive option for your office. Growing conditions differ for different ferns. Ferns do not need intense sunlight. Ideally indoor ferns will need some humidity and some indirect light. Suitable temperatures are 15-21 degrees Celsius.

     

    Plant name: Ivy

     

    Introduction:

    Ivy is a very popular indoor vine. It is suitable for many lighting conditions. It is a very low maintenance plant. Sufficient drainage is required and avoid using a big pot as it will keep the soil moist. Indirect sunlight is recommended. Ivy are known to tolerate moist and dry soil. This plant will be best for cooler temperatures- 10 to 24 degree celsius.

     

    Plant name: Supertunia Petunias

     

    Introduction:

    Petunia 'Supertunia Bordeaux' is a mounding trailing annual or perennial boasting masses of soft plummy-pink flowers adorned with rich plum-purple veins from late spring to the first frosts. Extremely easy to grow, Petunias thrive in full sun to part shade in moderately fertile, humus-rich, moist, well-drained soil.

     

    Plant name: Impatiens

     

    Introduction:

    Impatiens plants are one of the most popular annual flowers, due to their brightly colored blooms and their ability to grow in shady areas. Although technically tropical perennials, these plants are grown as annuals in all but the warmest regions (zones 10 to 12).

     

    Plant name: Trailing Pansies

     

    Introduction:

    These trailing plants grow 6 to 8 inches tall and spread 15 to 18 inches. Like Cool Waves, they're hardy to zone 5 and prefer full sun. For a wide range of colors, check out the Wonder Fall pansy series. Their large blooms come in shades of blue, lavender, rose and purple.
