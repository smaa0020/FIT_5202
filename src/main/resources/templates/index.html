    About

    This website that can help people contribute to bringing more natural spaces to Melbourne’s city in the following aspect:

    • Indoor / Outdoor garden design: bring green nature space to city
    • Personal planting knowledge: people can start gardening by themselves to create more nature
    • Protect current green spaces and biodiversity

    With the accelerated urban growth, the green spaces and biodiversity in Melbourne city is being lost, which may affect citizens’ health and well-being. To solve this problem, this project will provide a website which can contribute to bringing more natural spaces to Melbourne’s CBD
