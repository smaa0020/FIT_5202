    Type name: Indoor Atrium
    Introduction:

    If you have a spare room or a relatively open area to use, then the gorgeous indoor atrium park may be a good choice. You actually only need a coverage area that can be easily closed. You can even divide a small area on each floor and add some grids or fences on the edge to create the illusion of a closed garden house.

    Type name: Zen Garden
    Introduction:

    It is a minimalist dry landscape consisting of natural elements such as rocks, gravel, sand and wood, as well as a small amount of green plants. Each element has a deep symbolic meaning. The slanted patterned sand or gravel represents water, while larger rocks indicate islands, mountains, and animals. The emphasis on abstract concepts is intended to stimulate imagination and allow thoughts to drift.

    Type name: Vertical wall garden
    Introduction:

    A green wall is a vertical greening typology, where a vertical built structure is intentionally covered by vegetation. Green walls include a vertically applied growth medium such as soil, substitute substrate, or hydroculture felt; as well as an integrated hydration and fertigation delivery system. They are also referred to as living walls or vertical gardens.

    Type name: Indoor Water Garden
    Introduction:

    This is a fantastic water garden that incorporates your own fish friend into the mix! Using a plant, such as a fern, it’s roots can withstand complete water submersion can add an interesting garden element to your space. The roots of the plant create a natural food for your fish, while the fish’s waste acts as natural nutrition for the plant. It’s a symbiotic relationship that is not only really cool.

    Type name: Indoor Cactus Garden
    Introduction:

    Succulents are excellent indoor plants for gardeners of all levels. Normally, these drought-tolerant plants do not need a lot of water, so if you forget to give them a drink for a while, they are quite forgiving. Thus, Cactus gardens are very easly for creating because they require very little maintenance. Cacti can thrive under many different growing conditions.
