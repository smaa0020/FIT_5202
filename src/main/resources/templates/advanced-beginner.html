    • Advanced Beginner

    When we are familiar with the basic planting tools, we can start to try to grow our own garden. Here we will provide some basic knowledge of planting to help you have a basic understanding of planting. We hope that we can bring more greenery to the Melbourne CBD and give people more opportunities to contact nature.

    Step 1: Get the timing right

    First, according to the characteristics of the plants you choose, choose a suitable place for planting, such as temperature, indoors, outdoors, direct sunlight, shades, etc. When you buy seeds, there will be precautions for planting on the packaging of the seeds, please check carefully

     

     

     

    Step 2:The soil

    Choose soil designed for the growth of seedlings. Don't just use the soil in the garden, and don't reuse potting soil for indoor plants. Start with a fresh, sterile mixture to ensure healthy, disease-free seedlings.

     

     

     

    Step 3: Dig the soil & Add additional soil

    Check the seed pack to see how deep you should plant. Add additional soil, preferably organic soil to your garden. It makes a richer soil Avoid digging or treating the soil when it is wet to prevent compaction

     

     

     

    Step 4: Seed

    Carefully plant your seed or plant starts in the soil Please follow the instructions on the seed packaging to understand the sowing depth and interval of each seed Push the soil back, gently fix it, but don't compress it tightly

     

     

     

    Step 5: Water

    Plants need the right amount of water, do not too much or little. Wait until the soil is dry to water It's best to water deeply and less frequently than shallowly and more often so the roots of the plants grow deeper. Avoid keeping soil waterlogged or the roots of your flowering plants may rot.

     

     

     

    Step 6: Sunlight

    Grow your plants in the location so that get the enough sunlight Be careful, please choose the way of exposure to sunlight according to the habits of the plants

     

     

     

    Step 7: Trim your plants

    In order to ensure that your plants can grow up healthy, it is very important to trim the branches and leaves on time It's best to get any large amounts of pruning done during the spring or summer, when your plants are getting more sunlight and are actively growing.
